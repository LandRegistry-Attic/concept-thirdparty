Land Registry

If you would like Movr to act as your conveyancer you will need to give access to all the data related to the property held by the Land Registry.

You will need to agree that the following can view your documents:

  • Movr
  • The buyer
  • The buyer’s solicitor

You will also need to give permission to the buyer’s solicitor to change the registered owner details. This will happen after completion.
